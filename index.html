Design Principles
NO. 01

Systems over stories

A rulebook is more honest than a plot. We'd rather hand you a machine than tell you a myth.

NO. 02

Power without heroes

No chosen ones, no final bosses. Just structures, incentives, and the people caught inside them.

NO. 03

Games as working models

A good game simulates how something actually works — then lets you feel what that means.
